Bumblebee Amigurumi Pattern

Photo and Pattern by hooked by robin 

        
Yarn required:
Chunky yarn in bumblebee colours! Yellow, black and white
10mm safety eyes
Stuffing
4.5mm Crochet Hook (or whatever size suits your yarn, I always use a smaller hook than suggested for amigurumi projects as it keeps your stitches tighter)
Repeat pattern between the asterisks 
* …….. *  Pattern is worked in spirals, do NOT join at the end of each round
Pattern notes / explanations specific to each row 
(info inside bracket written in italics)
Stitch count in [  ] 
           
AMERICAN STITCH TERMS
Stitches / abbreviations used:  
MR = Magic Ring 
Inv dec = invisible decrease
Sc = Single Crochet 
Hdc = Half Double Crochet
Dc = Double Crochet
Ss = slip stitch
           
Using yellow to start:
R1 6sc into MR [6]
R2 inc*6                     [12]
R3 (sc, inc)* 6               [18]
R4 (2sc, inc)*6             [24]
R5 (3sc, inc)* 6                         [30]
R6 (4sc, inc)* 6                         [36]
R7 Sc in each stitch around     [36]
R8 Sc in each stitch around     [36]
R9 Sc in each stitch around     [36]
R10 Attach safety eyes and embroider face 
           
Change to black
           
R11 Sc in each stitch around     [36]
R12 Sc in each stitch around     [36]
R13 Change to yellow
R14 Sc in each stitch around     [36]
R15 Sc in each stitch around     [36]

Change to black

R16 Sc in each stitch around     [36]
R17 Sc in each stitch around     [36]
R18 Sc in each stitch around     [36]
R19 Sc in each stitch around     [36]

Change to black

R20 Sc in each stitch around     [36]
R21 Change to yellow
R22 (4sc, inv dec)* 6                  [30]
R23 (3sc, inv dec)* 6                  [24]
R24 (2sc, inv dec)* 6                  [18]

Stuff the body

R25 (sc, inv dec)* 6                    [12]
R26 (inv dec)* 6                          [6]
Cut yarn and run end around the outside loops of last 6 stitches. Synch closed and weave in end.
           
Wings – MAKE 2 
Wings are made with single crochet for round 1, half double crochet for round 2 and double crochet for round 3
Using white yarn
R1 6sc into MR               [6]           
R2 (Hdc inc)* 6            [12]            
R3 (1dc, dc inc)* 6        [18]           
Join with ss and cut yarn leaving long end to sew to body.            
Sew wings to the middle of the 2nd black stripe
